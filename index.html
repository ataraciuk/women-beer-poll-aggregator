IS LIGHT BEER REALLY
WOMEN'S CHOICE?

We believe women are mostly ignored by
beer companies as a target group.
There is also the prejudice that women prefer
light beer in spite of other types of beer.
At least the only commercials targeted
at women are about light beer.
This survey is for exploring this issue,
to validate or refute it.
